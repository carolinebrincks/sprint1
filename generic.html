Caroline Brincks

Caroline Brincks is a third-year Business Analytics & Information Systems student at the University of Iowa Tippie College of Business. She is graduating in May 2025.

Caroline is originally from Kansas City, Missouri and chose the University of Iowa for its major choices in the college of business, school spirit, and family history at the school. On campus, she has been involved in Tippie Senate, Delta Sigma Pi, Tippie Business Honor Society, the Honors College Diversity Council, and Women in Business. She has also held five jobs during her college career. After graduation, she will return to Iowa in the fall to continue her education at the College of Law.
